# 🤝 CODE OF CONDUCT

**Terra Ecosystem Community Guidelines**

---

## 🎯 OUR PLEDGE

We pledge to make participation in Terra Ecosystem a **harassment-free experience** for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, body size, disability
- Ethnicity, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education
- Nationality, personal appearance
- Race, religion, sexual identity

**Most importantly:** We prioritize **child safety** above all else.

---

## 👶 CHILD SAFETY FIRST

### Absolute Rules

1. **No content** that could harm children physically, psychologically, or intellectually
2. **No data collection** from children without verified parental consent
3. **No manipulation** of children through addictive design or dark patterns
4. **No exploitation** of children for commercial, political, or any other purpose

### Reporting Child Safety Concerns

**Priority Email:** a.abdukarimov@fractal-metascience.org  
**Response Time:** Within 24 hours  
**Action:** Immediate investigation and protection measures

---

## ✅ EXPECTED BEHAVIOR

- Be **respectful** and inclusive in all communications
- Be **collaborative** — we build together
- Be **transparent** about your intentions and affiliations
- Be **accountable** for your contributions and their impact
- Be **patient** with newcomers and different perspectives

---

## 🚫 UNACCEPTABLE BEHAVIOR

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Harassment | Offensive comments, intimidation, stalking |
| Discrimination | Exclusion based on identity, background, beliefs |
| Harmful Content | Misinformation, dangerous instructions, exploitation |
| Privacy Violations | Sharing private information without consent |
| Commercial Exploitation | Unsolicited advertising, data harvesting |
| Child Safety Violations | ANY content or behavior that could harm children |

---

## 📊 ENFORCEMENT

### Reporting Violations

**Email:** a.abdukarimov@fractal-metascience.org  
**Include:**
- Description of the incident
- Date, time, and location (URL, repository, etc.)
- Screenshots or evidence (if available)
- Your contact information

### Enforcement Actions

| Level | Action | Description |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| 🟡 Warning | Private notice | First minor violation |
| 🟠 Temporary Ban | 30-day suspension | Repeated or serious violation |
| 🔴 Permanent Ban | Forever | Severe violation, especially child safety |
| ⚫ Legal Action | Court/law enforcement | Criminal behavior, exploitation |

### Appeal Process

Banned members may appeal after 90 days by emailing a.abdukarimov@fractal-metascience.org with:
- Explanation of what happened
- What you've learned
- How you'll behave differently

## 🌍 CULTURAL RESPECT

Terra Ecosystem is global. We respect:

- **Religious diversity** — Islamic, Christian, Buddhist, Hindu, and all other traditions
- **Cultural traditions** — Local customs, languages, and practices
- **Educational approaches** — Different pedagogical philosophies
- **Family structures** — Various family models and parenting styles

### Religious Compatibility

We actively support integration with:
- Islamic values (Halal content, prayer times, family hierarchy)
- Christian values (Biblical integration, Sunday observance)
- Buddhist values (Mindfulness, non-violence, compassion)
- Hindu values (Dharma-based learning, respect for teachers)

---

## 🤖 AI AND TECHNOLOGY ETHICS

### AI Principles

1. **AI serves humans** — Never replaces human judgment on child safety
2. **Transparency** — AI decisions must be explainable
3. **No manipulation** — AI must not exploit psychological vulnerabilities
4. **Human oversight** — Critical decisions require human review

### Technology Standards

- Open source when possible
- Privacy by design
- Security by default
- Accessibility for all users
