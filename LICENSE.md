🎯 PREAMBLE
Terra Public License v1.0 (TPL-1.0) is a specialized license designed to protect children's rights in educational technologies. It combines open source principles with mandatory ethical standards for working with children's data and educational content.
🏛️ Legal Foundation
This license is based on:
UN Convention on the Rights of the Child (Articles 13, 16, 17, 28, 29)
GDPR Article 8 (Conditions applicable to a child's consent)
COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
UNESCO AI Ethics Recommendation
Terra Universal Convention v1.0
⚖️ License Philosophy
Terra Public License adheres to the "Child Safety First" principle:
Any use of licensed software must improve children's lives or, at minimum, do no harm to their physical, psychological, or intellectual development.
📋 DEFINITIONS
"Software" — source code, binary files, documentation, and any related materials from Terra Ecosystem licensed under TPL-1.0.
"Children" — persons under 18 years of age according to the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child, or under the age of majority in the relevant jurisdiction.
"Children's Data" — any information directly or indirectly related to children, including:
Personal identifiers
Educational information
Behavioral data
Biometric data
Communication records
Geolocation information
"Educational Purpose" — use for teaching, upbringing, developing children's intellectual abilities in formal or informal educational contexts.
"Commercial Use" — any use for profit, including selling products/services, advertising, collecting user data for monetization.
"Ethical Standards" — requirements of the Gefunden Ethical Framework and relevant international child protection standards.
"Derivative Work" — any modification, adaptation, translation, or extension of the Software.
